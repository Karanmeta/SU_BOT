The Mechanical Engineering Department at SCET focuses on the design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems.

Key Areas:
- Thermodynamics and Fluid Mechanics
- Machine Design and Manufacturing
- Mechatronics and Robotics
- CAD/CAM and Automation

Facilities:
Thermal Lab, Fluid Mechanics Lab, CNC Machining Lab, and Robotics Lab.

Curriculum integrates practical training, internships, and workshops with leading industries like L&T and Reliance.
