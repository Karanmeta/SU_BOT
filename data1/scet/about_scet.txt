Sarvajanik College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) is a premier engineering institute established in 1995 in Surat, Gujarat, India.
It is affiliated with Sarvajanik University and approved by AICTE.
SCET offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various branches of engineering and technology.
The college emphasizes innovation, technical research, and holistic student development.

SCET has well-equipped laboratories, digital classrooms, and a strong focus on project-based learning.
The institute maintains active collaborations with industries and promotes entrepreneurship through workshops, hackathons, and incubation programs.

Vision:
To be recognized as a leading institute that provides quality technical education and promotes research, innovation, and social responsibility.

Mission:
1. Impart value-based education that empowers students with technical and professional skills.
2. Foster innovation and entrepreneurship through collaborative learning.
3. Contribute to sustainable societal growth through research and ethical practices.
