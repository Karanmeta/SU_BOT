The Electronics and Communication Engineering Department at SCET focuses on modern electronics, communication systems, and embedded technologies.

Core Areas:
- Analog and Digital Electronics
- Communication Systems
- Embedded Systems and VLSI
- Signal Processing and IoT Devices
- Robotics and Control Systems

Faculty:
Led by experienced professors with research in RF Engineering, Digital Signal Processing, and Embedded Circuit Design.

Labs:
Electronics Workshop, Communication Lab, Microcontroller Lab, and IoT Lab.

The department emphasizes practical exposure through projects, mini-labs, and research-based assignments.
