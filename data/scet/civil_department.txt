The Civil Engineering Department at SCET offers courses in structural design, geotechnical engineering, environmental engineering, and transportation systems.

Focus Areas:
- Structural Engineering and Concrete Design
- Water Resources and Environmental Engineering
- Geotechnical Engineering and Soil Mechanics
- Transportation Engineering

Facilities:
Surveying Lab, Structural Engineering Lab, Environmental Engineering Lab.

Research and Projects:
The department undertakes projects related to smart city planning, sustainable construction, and waste-water treatment.
