The Information Technology Department at SCET focuses on software systems, artificial intelligence, and data-driven applications.
It was established to prepare students for careers in modern computing technologies and digital transformation.

Key Areas of Focus:
- Software Development and Full-Stack Engineering
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Data Science and Cloud Computing
- Web Technologies and Mobile App Development
- Cybersecurity and IoT

Laboratories:
The department houses specialized labs for AI, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Web Technologies, and IoT applications.

Faculty:
The department is headed by Dr. Vivaksha Jariwala.
Faculty members include Prof. Mita Parikh, Prof. Hiren Vavaiya, and Prof. Vivek Champenaria, who are actively involved in research and innovation.

Student Achievements:
Students regularly participate in Smart India Hackathon, GUJCOST projects, and publish papers in national and international conferences.

Curriculum:
Courses include Programming for Problem Solving, Data Structures, Database Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Web Development, and Big Data Analytics.
