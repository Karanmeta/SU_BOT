SCET has a dedicated Training and Placement Cell that assists students in securing internships and job opportunities.

Top Recruiters:
TCS, Infosys, L&T, ZS Associates, Capgemini, eInfochips, and Reliance Industries.

Average Package:
4.2 LPA for undergraduate programs with higher packages up to 10 LPA for top performers.

Placement Activities:
- Aptitude and soft-skill training sessions
- Resume-building and interview workshops
- Alumni mentoring programs
- On-campus and pooled campus drives

Placement Statistics:
Both IT and Computer Engineering departments have over 90% placement rates annually.
