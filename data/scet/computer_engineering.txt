The Computer Engineering Department at SCET emphasizes computing systems, hardware, and networking along with strong software foundations.

Key Areas of Focus:
- Computer Architecture and Operating Systems
- Microprocessors and Embedded Systems
- Networking and Cybersecurity
- Systems Programming and Compiler Design
- Database Management and Software Engineering

The department aims to produce technically skilled professionals who can work across hardware-software integration and system-level design.

Faculty:
Experienced faculty with expertise in systems programming, hardware design, and network security.
Faculty regularly contribute to research journals and academic conferences.

Facilities:
The department is equipped with Computer Architecture Labs, Networking Labs, and High-Performance Computing clusters for advanced projects.

Curriculum:
Includes courses such as Microprocessor and Interfacing, Computer Networks, Operating Systems, Compiler Construction, and Machine Learning.
